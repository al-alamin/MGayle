WHY TRAVEL WITH US

In his 18-­‐plus years as a personal trainer, Michael has realized physical training increases awareness of the inner self, the source of all success. His training system facilitates this connection within his clients, fueling life-­‐changing outcomes. As such,Michael is a dedicated advocate of creating wellness through awareness. Michael meets clients where they live, having worked with individuals from all fitness levels. When a client signs up to train and travel with Michael, he begins with a comprehensive analysis of where the client is at in relationship to the physical rigor required for the trip. He works with the client to ensure personal goals they desire for themselves are incorporated into their fitness plan, so that a mutual investment in the success of that plan is ensured. As the training regime continues, Michael measures success with objective criteria, agreed to in advance with the client. This fuels an increased level of investment in, and ownership of the results as progress is made. Training can be done in person, via Skype, or be sent to the client by video. As the trip start date nears, Michael begins to incorporate more outdoor training opportunities in to the training plan, thereby increasing more confidence in his clients as they visualize these activities directly translating to trip success. Finally, Michael provides expert support so that expectations, required equipment and logistics are communicated and clarified to mitigate any confusion and possible problems in advance of the trip. If needed, Michael can also assist clients with finding and buying trip gear. Throughout trip preparation, Michael solidly invests in his client’s success, opening and developing a differentiating dialog and relationship which translates to a life-­‐changing trip experience with a trusted friend and guide. As a result, clients are enabled to experience adventure travel from the inside out, allowing themselves to move into places within themselves they have perhaps never explored, as they physically experience the natural world. Michael further heightens this travel experience by carefully selecting wilderness and cultural opportunities that are difficult to find elsewhere in the industry. The end result is a WIN/WIN adventure of the highest magnitude. Michael offers several travel options for individuals and groups. For a full list of travel and training options, please review the Current Destinations and Policy links, respectively.

ABOUT MICHEAL GAYLE

Michael Gayle, founder of MGayle Outdoor Adventures, brings passion and enthusiasm to all of his endeavors, professional and personal. He treats every waking moment as an opportunity to learn, contribute and take action. This passion is the driving force behind how Michael sets and achieves his goals. This also enables him to help his clients achieve goals they set for themselves.

Michael’s athletic achievements include in-depth experience in outdoor survival, arctic & Antarctic sailing, personal training, Pilates, amateur boxing, kickboxing, martial arts, wrestling, skiing and cycling. He continues to develop and expand his fitness expertise; sharing this knowledge to support his clients as they strive to reach their own goals. As a former United States Marine, Michael cultivates a disciplined work ethic. His experiences in the military provided a strong foundation that helped him successfully operate MGayle Studio for 10 years. While Michael developed his personal fitness business to the point where he became a sought-after personal trainer, he challenged himself to find innovative ways to train his clients in the out-of-doors. As he developed his outdoor and survival expertise, he became more influential and skilled at planning and leading outdoor adventures, triggering the start of MGayle Outdoor Adventures. Michael doesn’t sit still and wait for life: he experiences it. His loyalty, professionalism, hospitality and expertise are unmatched. He is a catalyst of change, knowing that a deep emotional commitment is at the heart of achieving some of the most ambitious goals. His caring nature and ability to guide and motivate has produced life-changing shifts. Beyond physical goals, his work enables clients to achieve desired mental and spiritual changes through their training and adventure experiences. To Michael, his work with each client is, in reality, an investment in the larger community. Michael’s contributions to his community as a business owner and philanthropist has been acknowledged by the greater business community. He received the 2009 Business Person of the Year Award from the Highland Business Association and Saint Paul Sunrise Rotary. He is a graduate of the St. Paul Area Chamber of Commerce (SPACC) 10-month Leadership Saint Paul Program, and is a two-time finalist for the SPACC’s prestigious Deubener Award.
